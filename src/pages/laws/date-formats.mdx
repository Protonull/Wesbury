---
title: Formatting of Dates
subtitle: Issued by the Lord Admiral
type: STANDING ORDER
order: 900
current: true
---
On occasions where dates are shown on signs, such as, for example: displaying
when a build was constructed, commemorative signs, or dereliction notices, then
the following schemas are allowed:

- `Month DD, YYYY`

- `DD Month YYYY`

- `YYYY-MM-DD`
